# 希腊字母

## I.小写希腊字母

$$
alpha \to \alpha \\
beta \to \beta \\
gamma \to \gamma \\
delta \to \delta \\
epsilon \to \epsilon \\
eta \to \eta \\
theta \to \theta \\
vartheta \to \vartheta \\
lambda \to \lambda \\
mu \to \mu \\
xi \to \xi \\
pi \to \pi \\
sigma \to \sigma \\
omega \to \omega \\
phi \to \phi \\
varphi \to \varphi
$$

---

## II.大写希腊字母

$$
Alpha \to \Alpha \\
Beta \to \Beta \\
Gamma \to \Gamma \\
Delta \to \Delta \\
Theta \to \Theta \\
Phi \to \Phi \\
Omega \to \Omega
$$
